what we do

community outreach programs

WeastZaga is a group founded on the basis of the community it main purpose to help in making a better place for every needy people to feel part of the society. the groupd helps people in the sosciety that is KIbera in terms social and financial support. the needy are given social amenities and sometimes provided basic needs to help them better their lives and make them comfortable.


Youth Empowerment Programs

a larger population of the the youth from the slums are litterrally unemployed. for this reason the group has constantly been on the look out for the opportunities that will help atleast help raise skills and provide the necessary requirements that will see the youth prosper. these programs include teaching them on how to apply for jobs and come up with world class CVs that will help them get them a job and also help in getting business skills and some of the end up getting a loan to set up a business.


slums clean up sessions

the group commits its self in delivering cleanness in the slum. this program is done in two ways- one the group cleans the trenches twice a month so that water can flow smoothly without getting stuck anywhere so as to avoid stagnant water that will lead to increased mosquitos. the second way is the group provided plastic bags to households which is collected everysunday but this one comes with a fee and every household is required to pay ksh20 every month

WeastZaga profile

WEASTZAGA is an independent registered youth group and nonprofit civil society organization based in Nairobi county. WEASTZAGA prime motto is capacity building and services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society. In 2014 a group of educated, dynamic and like-minded youth working in the development sector, got together to establish an organization, for addressing the most urgent and pressing needs of the people of KIBERA. True to this spirit the group was named weast and east gonzaga (WEASTZAGA).

WeastZaga Mission Statement

The mission of WEASTZAGA is to contribute to the education of young people, through a value system based, to help build a better world where people are self-fulfilled as individuals and play a constructive role in society .
